### **What to Use:**

- Clear face, good lighting
- Solid or blurred background
- Smile and look approachable
- Dress professionally (business casual)

### **Specs:**
- Size: 400x400 pixels minimum
- Format: JPG or PNG
- File size: Under 8MB

8. **Creator Mode**: Enable for better visibility
